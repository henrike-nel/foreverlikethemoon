The story behind the name "Foreverlikethemoon"

The name refers to Psalm 89:37. In the verse God compares the ​timespan of his covenant with David to the ​eternality of the moon.  Marriage is a sacred covenant between a husband and his wife to endure, just like the moon, forever.

And just like the moon is a “faithful witness in the sky” marriage is designed to be the display of God.
