ForeverLikeTheMoon

About

I am pleased to introduce to you the ultimate wedding planner.  The A to Z (literally) on what to do before you say I do.  It has 32 sections, over 200 pages and is the perfect size to carry everywhere.

The purpose of this wedding planner is to assist those want to plan their own big dream wedding day, but don’t know where to start or when to do what.

About Me

My name is Henrike and I'm the creative driving force behind ForeverLikeTheMoon.

I spend most of my days as a software engineer for a company called BASH in Cape Town. Graphic design has always been a passion of mine and except for some freelance work here and there, this is my first big "passion" project.

Being the obsessive planner that I am, I wanted to make sure that when I get engaged, that I will have everything I need to plan the perfect wedding. A planner to make sure that the best day of my life will run smoothly and all processes leading up to it: stress free. 
That is how it all began.

Sections

Accommodation

Who will stay on-site (if accommodation is available) and what accommodation suggestions is close to the venue for friends and family.

Administration

Keeping track of scheduled appointments, details about marriage license obtainment and name changing checklist.

Attire

Attire for the groom, wedding party and parents of the bride and groom.

Bar

What are the different options for bar services and what liquor selection do we want to be available. 

Bouquet

A checklist for the flowers needed for the wedding ceremony. 

Budget

Who pays for what? Budget planner and expense tracker.

Catering 

What style do we want the dinner to be served and what should the menu look like? Who has special ​dietary requirements and what will they be served as a substitution?

Decor

Decor checklists and setup notes.

Entertainment

Details about entertainment for the evening and who will be giving speeches and toasts.

FAVOURS

Details about wedding favours for the guests.

Guest list

Guests we consider inviting to the wedding. Who should I add and who should I cut from the guest list? 

honeymoon

Packing list and Accommodation and flight details.

itInerary 

Itinerary for the big day.

Job delegation  

Delegating specific responsibilities 

KITS

What should be in the troubleshooting stash that has saved many a day. 

LOGISTICS

What happens when? 

MUSIC

Music playlists to be played during specific events.

nourishment

Sunless tanning schedule and  grooming appointments. 

Officiant

Who will officiate our marriage and guide us through the legal requirements and paper work? 

Party

Details about the wedding party of the bride and groom. 

Photography 

Details about the photographer you booked for the wedding and photo checklist of specific photos wanted taken during different parts of the day.

Questions

What wedding traditions do you want to honour? What will your colour palette look like? 

Registry

A curated list of gifts to guide the wedding guests in buying wedding gifts.

Seating Chart

Different types of table layouts for your wedding reception and planning on who will sit where.

timeline 

A suggested guide on what to do when (over a timespan of 12 months).

USHERs

Who will be in charge of seating guests and directing them to parking lots, bathrooms, dining rooms, and other wedding sites?

Vendors

Details about vendors we are considering for the wedding. 

Venue

Venue details and comparisons.

Wedding dress  

Different sleeve styles, dress shapes and necklines.  

(E)xit

Some send-off ideas and admin. 

yippee

Some final details about your dream day. 

zones

The different zones (areas) we will have at our wedding reception.
